## Why This Journal?

- Tvedt (2010) — the paper we directly build on — was published here
- Interdisciplinary readership receptive to NLP + econometrics methodology
- Strong Great Divergence tradition (directly engages Pomeranz, Broadberry, Tvedt)
- Less heavy econometric scrutiny than pure cliometrics journals → our collapsed DiD power limitation is less of a liability
- Canal illustrations (England vs. Asia) directly serve the journal's comparative global framing
